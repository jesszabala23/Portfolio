about.



I started my career as a designer in production & digital marketing but was left
wanting to perform in an industry that can make viable differences in my community.
That is when I decided to change gears into TECH. In five weeks, with the help of
TechBridge, I managed to teach myself HTML, CSS, JavaScript & Jquery and won first
place in their Front-End Web-Development Contest for my Atlanta Lifestyle website that
showcased all the wonderful places Atlanta has to offer.

As a first generation American I am a strong believer in diversity and inclusion in
the workplace as it is central to attract talented co-workers, and innovations.
Having worked 8+ years in industries that are highly Male run I thrive to see higher
representation of women in C-suite level positions.
